Old Town School of Music Poster
Size: 20x30"
Tools: Adobe Indesign, Adobe Illustrator

This poster is for the Old Town School of Music. With this project, we were restricted to one color and one typeface. With the main name as the emphasis, I decided to use the theme of distortion to portray movement, sound, and interest.
